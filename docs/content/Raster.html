Raster Data Model

Represents space continuously as a grid of cell (aka pixels)

-A simple but storage intensive format.

Raster Data Model

Each cell can have only one value. Multiple raster (bands) can be combined to yield more information

-This is how digital photos work. The store three separate bands (R,B,& B)

Resolution

Cell size dictates resolution.

10 m cells are:

-10 x 10 m (100 m2)

1 m cells are:

-1 x 1 m (1 m2)

Extent

The number of rows and columns given the cells size, determines the extent (aka domain or bounds) of a raster image.

Extent

1 m cell size, 5 rows x 5 columns:

Total area 25 m2

10 m cell size, 5 rows x 5 columns:

Total area 2500 m2
